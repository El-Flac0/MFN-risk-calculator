Results grouped and summed by generic name, separately for Part B and Part D. Each part loads two datasets (2019–2023 and 2024–2025 ★), merged per generic name. Loads once per session then cached.

Part B covers drugs administered in physician/outpatient settings. 2019–2023 from historical dataset; ★ 2024–2025 from newer dataset, merged per generic name.
Part D covers retail/mail-order prescriptions (Overall aggregate row only). 2019–2023 from historical dataset; ★ 2024–2025 from newer dataset, merged per generic name.
Spending summed across all brand variants sharing the same generic name, within each part separately.
△ 2026 projected via linear regression on 2019–2025 actuals, floored at $0.
★ IRA MFP = drug subject to IRA Maximum Fair Price negotiation (shown with cycle). Overrides all other badges.
🌐 GLOBE = Part B projected 2026 ≥ $100M (suppressed when drug is present and multi-source in Part D). 🛡 GUARD = Part D projected 2026 ≥ $69M (single manufacturer only). ⚖ MULTI-SOURCE = drug has >1 manufacturer in Part D; shown on both the Part D card and the corresponding Part B card. Source: data.cms.gov.
