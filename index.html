Matches any part of the brand or generic name across both datasets. Each dataset loads once on first use (~5–15 s), then stays cached for instant re-searches.

Part B covers drugs administered in physician offices, hospital outpatient settings, and other clinical settings. Includes HCPCS code and description.
Part D covers retail and mail-order prescription drugs. Includes manufacturer-level rows plus an "Overall" aggregate.
Tot_Spndng = total Medicare gross drug spending (USD). N/A = suppressed or unreported data for that year. Source: data.cms.gov.
